2. Schemes

You can apply for 2 of the 4 Generalist Fast Stream schemes at the same time. There are rules about how you can combine the schemes. To apply for Central Departments, Diplomatic Service, Houses of Parliament or Science and Engineering you’ll first need to apply for the Generalist Fast Stream scheme and then select your preferred options once you’ve passed the online tests.

Degree requirements and timetable for applications

You may need to meet other requirements, eg nationality. See the relevant scheme for more information.

4. Application process

Step 1

Submit your application

Once you know which scheme or schemes you're interested in, use the digital service to create an account and apply.

This should take no longer than 30 minutes to complete.

Step 2

1 week to complete

Take the online tests

After submitting your application form, we'll email you a link to 2 online tests.

  • Verbal
  • Numerical reasoning

Step 3

2 weeks to complete

Complete the e-tray exercise and video interview

We'll send you a link to an e-tray exercise to complete and a link to do a recorded video interview.

Step 4

In 4 - 8 weeks

Attend an assessment centre

If you pass the online exercises, you'll be invited to attend an assessment centre.

You'll complete the following exercises at the assessment centre:

  • leadership exercise
  • analysis exercise
  • group exercise

Next steps

We aim to let you know the outcome within 12 weeks of submitting your application.

If you're successful, you will be offered a place on the Fast Stream based on your scheme preferences.

For selected schemes, you may need to take an additional test. If this applies to you, someone will contact you.

If your pre-appointment checks are successful, you'll start your new role in September 2017.

  2. Take the online assessments

    The online assessments consist of:

    1. Exercises

      You'll need to complete 2 online exercises which consist of several questions to test competency and situational judgement. You'll have 7 days to complete these exercises.

      Find out more about the online exercises

    2. E-tray

      If you pass the online exercises, you'll then need to complete the E-tray assessment. This is a time-sensitive inbox simulation, to see how you work under pressure and in a number of different scenarios.

      Find out more about the E-tray

    3. Video interview

      If you pass the E-tray assessment, you'll be invited to do the online video interview. This is an Asynchronous video interview, where you'll first see an assessor asking a pre-recorded question and you then record yourself giving an answer.

      Find out more about the video interview

    Video interview

    As you've completed the Summer Diversity Internship Programme (SDIP) the only assessment you'll have to do is the online video interview. This is an Asynchronous video interview, where you'll first see an assessor asking a pre-recorded question and you then record yourself giving an answer.

    Find out more about the video interview

  3. Attend an assessment centre

    If you pass the online exercises, you'll be invited to attend an assessment centre.

    Find out more about the assessment day

    Final selection board

    You must go through a final selection process when applying for some Fast Stream schemes. We’ll ask you to attend a Final Selection Board if you reach the required standard at the assessment centre and you’ve applied for one of these schemes:

    • Diplomatic Service
    • Houses of Parliament
    • Science and Engineering
    • Digital and Technology
    • European
    • Government Communication Service

    Find out more about the final selection board

  4. Your final results

    We aim to let you know the outcome of your assessment by December 2016.

    Find out more about what happens after you've been accepted for the Fast Stream
